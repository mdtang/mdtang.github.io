Problem

As a new owner of a small day spa in Downtown Ann Arbor, my client needed help with attracting customers and generating sufficient revenue to make a significant profit. I have doubled her business' revenue over the past four months and I continue to help her expand the reach of her customer-base across Ann Arbor and surrounding cities.
