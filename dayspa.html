Problem

As a new owner of a small day spa in Downtown Ann Arbor, my client needed help with attracting customers and generating sufficient revenue to make a significant profit. However, she did not speak fluent English and faced difficulty in using technology to post online advertisements and connect with her clients via the internet.

Process and Solution

I accepted her generous request for me to work as her business development manager and translator. I designed and currently maintain her business' WordPress website to connect with customers. I also distribute ads on online platforms such as Craigslist, create promotional posters and monthly business plans, and manage property paperwork. I have doubled her business' revenue over the past four months and I continue to help her expand the reach of her customer-base across Ann Arbor and surrounding cities.
