HCI / Mobile App Research


UMSI HCI/Mobile App Research

Conduct interviews, analyze user behavior, and develop human-computer interaction skills in the Social Innovations Group (SIG) for the Understand the Benefits of People-Nearby Applications (PNAs) project.

I work to understand the benefits of people-nearby apps (PNAs) such as Tinder, as well as examining how visual design of an application can affect user experience. Specifically, I study the behavior and experiences of immigrants to understand how PNAs can suit their different needs.
