HCI / Mobile App Research


UMSI HCI/Mobile App Research

Conduct interviews, analyze user behavior, and develop human-computer interaction skills in the Social Innovations Group (SIG) for the Understand the Benefits of People-Nearby Applications (PNAs) project.

I interview research subjects about their perceptions of and experiences with PNAs, and how they think these apps can benefit them academically, socially, and mentally. I also teach participants who have no previous experience with PNAs how these apps are designed and how to use them, which allows my research team to assess how using these apps affects them.
