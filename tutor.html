    Community Service - ESL and College Prep Tutoring

Interpreter, Mentor, Tutor

Daily Academic Tutoring for ESL Student

Problem

My high school is known as one of the best performing arts schools in the city of Philadelphia. However, we do not receive sufficient funding from the school district. This has resulted in teachers being forced to leave and resources being cut by significant margins. An ESL student from China who struggled with the English language barrier and public speaking sought help from teachers. As a temporary solution, the school hired a temporary ESL assistant who was unable to assist him for more than a few hours a week and did not speak his native language.

Process and Solution

As a close friend of his who spoke his native language, I saw the perfect opportunity to tutor and mentor him, which would save the school tens of thousands of dollars and would make it easier for him to seek help for his schoolwork as well as any personal issues he faced. To assist this student, I translated lectures for him, tutored him on grammar and writing, and taught him public speaking skills both in and out of class.

After almost four years, he can now speak fluent English and no longer faces issues with public speaking.

September 2011 - June 2015

Philadelphia, PA

SAT and College Preparation and Readiness Workshop

Problem

Due to a significant shortage of financial resources, my high school lacked a robust college preparation program for students. I sought to fix that problem.

Process and Solution

I founded a workshop program that prepared eleventh grade students for standardized testing and college applications. I also advanced students’ knowledge on application deadlines and test and essay writing strategies. Current twelfth grade students have continued this program even after I graduated.
