    Customer Service - South Quad Dining

Problem

South Quad is the busiest dining hall at the University of Michigan, and often there are not enough workers to support the significant numbers of diners on any given day. U of M aims to hire more student workers to increase the efficiency of each station so diners can have the best dining experience, especially for students before and after class.

Process and Solution:

I, along with other employees, worked during the summer to ensure the cleanliness of the dining hall as well as the availability of all food stations. Furthermore, I cooperated with other dishroom employees to make sure that all dishes and silverware are thoroughly cleaned and put back to various stations for diners to use.
